## Style Notes (apply across all images)

- **Background**: Deep navy (#0a0f1e) or dark charcoal (#111827)
- **Primary accent**: Microsoft blue (#0078d4) and electric cyan (#00b4d8)
- **Secondary accents**: Violet (#7c3aed), teal (#0d9488), amber-gold (#f59e0b) used sparingly
- **Texture**: Subtle grid or hex mesh overlays at 5–10% opacity
- **Typography**: No readable text in any image (avoids prompt-generation artefacts)
- **Style**: Between enterprise SaaS marketing illustration and technical architecture diagram — not photorealistic, not cartoon
- **Aspect ratio**: Featured image 16:9 (1200×675 or 1600×900); section images 16:9 or 3:2
- **No Microsoft logos or wordmarks** — use abstract equivalents (constellation of app icons, M365-style colour palettes)
